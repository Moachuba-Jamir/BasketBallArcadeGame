Things to know

Welcome, here are some game rules to get you started!

  • You get 2 points for every score.
  • There are 3 Game modes. 30 seconds | 60 seconds | 90 seconds.
  • Compete yourself with other players and top the leaderboards
  • Every time you obtain a high score, you will be prompted to enter your name and department details. This user information will be reflected on the leaderboards.
  • Every game mode has it's own high scores and leaders.
  • You can press the Start to start playing.
  • Make sure to have fun!
